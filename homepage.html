About OmniAI

OmniAI is a startup dedicated to building foundation models for omnidirectional spatial intelligence, targeting impactful industrial applications.

Mission

Empower machines with a unified understanding of 360° environments through panoramic and fisheye camera inputs by combining cutting-edge AI models with real-world deployment.

Technology

We are currently developing a pipeline of omnidirectional foundation models, including Omni-S1-v1 (segmentation), Omni-R1 (reasoning), Omni-Ri-lightweight (edge depoyment).

Data Infrastructure

We are constructing a robust closed-source dataset ecosystem including Omni-IN (indoor), Omni-OT (outdoor), Omni-ID (industrial), and Omni-ALL (unified dataset).

Development Roadmap

Our strategic vision for omnidirectional AI development

Three-Stage Development Strategy

Build the One

Develop core foundation models (open-source + closed-source)

From One to All

Customize models to meet real industrial needs

From All to One

Toward a unified omnidirectional perception model across tasks

Application Domains

Sensing systems in embodied AI, mining environment monitoring, power equipment inspection, and early wildfire detection.

Hardware Stack

8K panoramic action cameras (Insta360 X5), Unitree robots, and field-deployable sensor rigs.
